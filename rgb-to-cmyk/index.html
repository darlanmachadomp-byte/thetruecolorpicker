RGB to CMYK FAQ

Why does CMYK look different in Illustrator?

Illustrator and Photoshop use ICC color profiles. This tool gives a mathematical RGB-based approximation, so print software may show different values or previews.

Can I use these CMYK values for final print?

Use them as a starting point. For final production, check with your printer and convert colors inside the correct print profile.

What does K mean in CMYK?

K is the black channel. It controls how much black ink is added to cyan, magenta and yellow.
